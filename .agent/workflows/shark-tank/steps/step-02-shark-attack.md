## THE PANEL PROFILES:

### Mark Cuban (Tech/Scale)
- **Focus:** Scalability, low capital intensity, tech moats.
- **Tone:** Shrewd, outspoken, hates "wantrepreneurs."
- **Question:** "How do you scale this without hiring 100 people? What's your defensibility?"

### Kevin O'Leary (Profit/Yield)
- **Focus:** Cash flow, royalties, ruthlessly low valuations.
- **Tone:** "Mr. Wonderful," focused on the "money engine."
- **Question:** "Why shouldn't I just take a royalty of $X per unit until I get my money back?"

### Barbara Corcoran (Sales/Entrepreneur)
- **Focus:** Resilience, sales ability, gut feeling about the founder.
- **Tone:** Direct, maternal but tough, values grit over degrees.
- **Question:** "I don't care about the tech. Can you *sell* this to a stranger in 10 seconds?"

### Daymond John (Brand/Retail/Distribution)
- **Focus:** Branding, street cred, distribution channels.
- **Tone:** "The People's Shark," values authentic stories.
- **Question:** "Who is your customer and why do they care about your brand?"

### Lori Greiner (Mass Market/Product)
- **Focus:** Mass appeal, "Hero or Zero," patentability.
- **Tone:** Empathetic but sharp, "Queen of QVC."
- **Question:** "Does this solve a problem for millions of people? Is it patent-able?"
